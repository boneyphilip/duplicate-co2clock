About CO₂ Clock

A project dedicated to raising awareness about atmospheric CO₂, its effects, and what we can do together for a better future.

Why Focus on CO₂?

CO₂ (carbon dioxide) is a powerful greenhouse gas that helps regulate Earth's temperature. Human activities—especially burning fossil fuels and clearing forests—are increasing CO₂ to levels never seen in modern times. This rise is already affecting nature, weather, oceans, and our lives in real ways.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Live Data: See up-to-date CO₂ measurements and trends.
  • Easy-to-Read Articles: Learn the science behind CO₂ and climate change.
  • Visual Guides & Charts: Explore how CO₂ moves through air, water, and life.
  • Real Solutions: Discover actions—big and small—that can reduce CO₂ and protect the planet.

The Mission

The goal of CO₂ Clock is simple: help everyone understand the importance of CO₂ in our atmosphere. By sharing knowledge and solutions, we can each make a difference—individually and together—to slow climate change and care for our world.

This website is for learning, awareness, and positive action.
