Climate Solutions

Renewable Energy Transition

Shifting to renewable energy sources could reduce global emissions by 65% by 2050.

  • Solar energy costs dropped 90% in the last decade
  • Wind power could provide 20% of global electricity by 2030
  • Renewables create 3x more jobs per dollar than fossil fuels
Forest Conservation

Protecting and restoring forests could remove 5.8 billion tons of CO₂ annually.

  • Forests absorb 2.6 billion tons of CO₂ each year
  • Deforestation accounts for 11% of global CO₂ emissions
  • Reforestation could capture 25% of current emissions
